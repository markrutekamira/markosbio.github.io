About Us

Markos Bio is an early-stage biotechnology venture focused on exploring biocatalytic and bio-based approaches to sustainability, research-driven innovation, and scientific solutions for societal challenges.

We are currently in the research and concept-development phase, working to identify scalable biological pathways that can support a circular bioeconomy and other health and sustainability initiatives.

Why Sustainability?

Environmental and health challenges remain among the most urgent issues in Africa. Markos Bio is motivated by the need for locally relevant, scientifically grounded solutions that combine environmental responsibility, technological feasibility, and social impact.

Our Focus Areas

  • Biocatalysis and microbial systems for sustainability
  • Plastic waste and circular bioeconomy concepts
  • Bio-based materials and biodegradable innovations
  • Research translation for societal impact
