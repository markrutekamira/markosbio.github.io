About

Markos Bio is an early-stage biotechnology initiative focused on learning, research exploration, and concept development in biocatalysis, bio-based materials, and sustainable innovation.

This platform documents ideas, scientific interests, and future projects as they evolve.

Current Focus

  • Biocatalysis & microbial systems
  • Plastic waste circularity concepts
  • Bio-based materials and sustainability
  • Health & science-driven innovation in Africa

Status

No products are being offered at this time. Markos Bio is currently in an exploratory and learning phase.
