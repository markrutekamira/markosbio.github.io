About Us

Markos Bio is an early-stage biotechnology venture exploring biocatalytic and bio-based approaches to sustainability, research-driven innovation, and scientific solutions for societal challenges.

We are currently in the research and concept-development phase, identifying scalable biological pathways that can support a circular bioeconomy and other health and sustainability initiatives.
